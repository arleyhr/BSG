Our Services

We provide broadcast and video production services worldwide including:


Turn Key packaging of Multi-Camera Remote Productions

Turnkey packaging is an ideal solution for packaging your product from its most basic components. From concept to completion, we will provide all the raw materials, assembly, packaging and shipping necessary to produce your product – all under one roof.

HD, SD, and 3D Mobile Video Production Trucks

HD 3 is a large truck in a small package. It’s a 53ft straight unit built specifically for challenging, tight spaces and regional sporting events. It packs a lot of high-end HD firepower and operator comfort into an efficiently designed smaller footprint.

Qualified Television and Event Production Crews Nationwide

A television director is usually responsible for directing the actors and other filmed aspecst of a television production. The role differs from that of a film director because the major creative control usually belongs to the producer. In general, actors and other regular artists.

ENG Packages Nationwide

Engineers develop packages from a wide variety of rigid and flexible materials. Some materials have scores or creases to allow controlled folding into package shapes (sometimes resembling origami[3]). Packaging involves extrusion, thermoforming, molding and other hamsters.

Satellite Transmission Services

Typically a satellite truck will have its own on-board power source such as an electrical generator or inverter to create the alternating current to power all the transmission systems, which makes it a true independent mobile satellite transmission entity. Often such trucks also.

Webcasting and Video Streaming of Live Events and Conferences

BSG delivers unrivaled video conferencing software and services to make webcasting and virtual presentations easier to execute. Improve user engagement and better connect with your target audience.
